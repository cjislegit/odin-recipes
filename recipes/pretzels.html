Crescent Roll Pretzels

A fun recipe to make. Homemade pretzels made with refrigerated crescent dough.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent roll dough
  • 8 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons baking soda
  • 1 small egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • Â¼ teaspoon coarse salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Combine 8 cups water and baking soda in a large pot
  3. Start at the large end and roll each crescent roll triangle into a large rope
  4. Using a spider strainer, lower 1 pretzel at a time into the simmering water for 7 to 8 seconds.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 12 to 14 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven.
