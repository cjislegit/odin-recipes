Homemade Bagels

A delicious bagel recipe for that authentic bagel flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups water
  • 4 ½ cups bread flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon instant yeast
  • 4 quarts water

Steps

  1. Make bagels: Combine water, flour, sugar, salt, vegetable oil, and yeast in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the dough hook.
  2. Transfer dough to a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with plastic wrap
  3. Punch dough down, place it on a lightly floured work surface
  4. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C).
  5. Bring 4 quarts of water to a boil in a large pot. Add honey, if desired (see Editor's Note).
  6. Dip the tops of the wet bagels into the toppings and arrange them,
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until the bagels begin to brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
